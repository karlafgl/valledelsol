Welcome to Valle Del Sol, Los Cabos, Baja Mexico.

Let us first express our gratitude to you in allowing us to introduce our new community and family here in Cabo San Lucas Mexico. My name is John Buckley and my wife Blanca are excited to be creating this new family themed community here in our home town of Cabo San Lucas

Nestled in the valley high above the crowded communities of Cabo and far from the noise of the nightlife. Valle del Sol (meaning valley of the sun) is an Eco Friendly residential community that focuses on self sustainability. A true focus and passion for reducing our carbon footprint and using the lush land in the Baja peninsula to carve out your own peace of paradise. Inspired by the classic farm home open concept, the houses are high quality builds with large kitchens and spacious living spaces. Each home overlooks the Cabo bay and the famous Cabo Arch complemented with a sprawling view of the city and the Baja landscape

The popular Baja quote “No bad days” applies to the lifestyle of this community, a positive upbeat community that loves outdoor activities such as hiking, mountain biking, off road vehicles, horseback riding, boating and much more. Many enjoy heading down to the beaches for the day and bringing their lunches, and others enjoy grilling a thick steak up on their ocean view terrace. Our family orientated community features like minded owners who respect their natural surroundings, people's privacy and living in harmony with one another.

Focusing on outdoor living areas and activities, the community also has a communal vegetable patch where residents can plant fruits and vegetables for a healthier lifestyle.

Valle del Sol offers the best of both worlds, privacy and quiet natural surroundings with mountain and wild desert nature to be explored at your doorstep or enjoy the hustle and bustle of the city and its activities and shopping within a 10 minute drive.

We are excited to find more like minded clients who appreciate our community and its lifestyle.

Valle del Sol is a 41 lot eco-development on 7.2 Acres (2.9 Hec) that offers 1, and 2 bedroom custom Baja style ranch & farmhouses, built to order on spacious 400-500 m2 lots landscaped with native trees, cactus, & plants in a sustainable community, where nature is at your doorstep and your home & lifestyle is in harmony with the natural Baja environment. For the lucky few, there's nothing like it in Los Cabos.

The 2 bedroom 2 bath model has a spacious floor plan with both bedrooms on the main floor for easier accessibility. There are two spacious en suites and the master has a walk-in closet. The living room and dining room areas are quite large and offer a great open concept living space due to the loft style second floor. The loft can be used as a guest bedroom or a tv room or even an office, this area has the best view of the property as it has a large terrace that overlooks the Cabo Arch.

The Community

The community is one of the main attractions of Valle del Sol. Separating itself from other developments in Cabo, our community is tightly knit and focused on friendliness and no intruding on your neighbour’s peace and tranquility, and a relaxed living environment.

As an Eco Friendly and Sustainable Residential Development, Valle del Sol’s community is more of a family than it is a residence as we look forward to living together and enjoying what Mexico has to offer.

Water and electric:

Valle del Sol uses Municipal Water, provided from the local aquifers. Valle del Sol offers both CFE and solar energy options.

Locations and Views

Valle del Sol is located 10 minutes past the Los Cabos Costco in the hills of El Tezal, with panoramic views of Lands End, the pacific Ocean and the city of San Lucas. The property is complemented by spectacular ocean views by day and sparkly starry night and dancing city lights at night.
