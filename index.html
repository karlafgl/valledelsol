About us

Valle del Sol

If the reason’s you came to Cabo was to get away from the city and pressures of life north of the border, and find a home where you could relax and enjoy the tranquility of Baja lifestyle with all it has to offer, then Valle Del Sol might be the little corner of heaven you’ve been looking for.

Nestled in the Valley of the Sun, where the tropical desert meets the lush foothills of the Sierra La Laguna, just 3 miles from town overlooking and Cabo bay & the Sea of Cortez from the heights of El Tezal, Valle Del Sol is the only gated ranch style community in Los Cabos with 24 hour security and easy access to everything in Cabo.

Read More

Valle del Sol is a 41 lot Eco-development on 7.2 Acres (2.9 Hec) that offers 1, 2, & 3 bedroom custom Baja style ranch & farmhouses, built to order on spacious 400-500 m2 lots landscaped with native trees, cactus, & plants in a sustainable community, where nature is at your doorstep and your home & lifestyle is in harmony with the natural Baja environment.

Homes at Valle del Sol feature a 2 story design with a 2nd level loft in addition to the bedrooms with lots of natural lighting, stain glass windows, solar power systems (with full CFE backup), municipal water supply from the local desert aquifers, high capacity water storage cisterns, 2nd floor view & garden terraces, and covered parking. And since all homes are built to order for each owner, the Valle Del Sol developer offers many additional options to customize your home including fire pits, plunge pools & Jacuzzis, extra terraces, Baja BBQs, custom vegetable & spice gardens, renewable irrigation systems, garages and additional storage.
